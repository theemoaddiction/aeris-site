PROPHECY

Aeris Mandrake is an electronic-leaning indie artist whose work blurs raw emotion with synthetic texture, folding distorted pop hooks into messier, darker undercurrents. Her songs move between intimacy and abrasion, pairing vulnerable melodies with industrial edges, synth-driven atmospheres, and the restless energy of indie rock and emo.

Signed to Sodalite Media, Aeris emerged from the same creative orbit as Jet-Set Stereo, who first encountered her singing quietly to herself in the back of a Spencer's, half-buried under fluorescent light and novelty displays. Since then, she has become a frequent collaborator within the Jet-Set Stereo universe, channeling fractured memories and late-night impulses into music that feels both immediate and strangely displaced.
